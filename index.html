About Us

Uvanest Company.

At Uvanest Capital Limited, we are an experienced team of professionals that combine a passion for Client Satisfaction and Industry research on the Fixed Income Markets. The company with an operational base in Lagos, the financial and business hub of Nigeria, was incorporated under the Company and Allied Matters Act 1990 in January 2016 and commenced operations in April 2016. Over the period, our trading activities and excellence has positioned us to maintain an array of clientele base which we serve using our service mantra ‘integrity through service’ as our watch word leading usa very vibrant trading relationships with key players in the Nigerian Financial Market space.
